    THE STORY

    Sup. We truly believe that life should be anything but boring. Marilyn Monroe said it best: “it's better to be absolutely ridiculous than absolutely boring”. She’s right. We love out of the ordinary people, those that stand out and aren’t afraid of it. What better day is there to express yourself than a Friday?


    FiestaFriday was born from a deep passion for good music, funky shirts and drinks. Think of us as the GQ for a Friday night… Just without the celebs (Jeff Goldblum, we’re open to a chat). We will be changing the content biweekly to keep it fresh.


    So add that playlist, buy that shirt and pour that drink because we’re unapologetically loud and you should be too.
