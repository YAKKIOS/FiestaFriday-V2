    03. DRINK

    BLACKBERRY MOJITO

  • ICE
  • 6-8 mint leaves
  • 3-4 blackberries
  • 25ml sugar syrup
  • 25ml lime juice
  • 50ml Bacardi White Rum
  • club soda

    • In a shaker, muddle lime juice, mint leaves, blackberries, and simple syrup until pulverized. Add rum and ice and shake for 10 seconds. Pour contents into a glass (no straining) and top with club soda. Stir and garnish with mint leaves and blackberry.
